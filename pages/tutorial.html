Contracting Party Tutorial

Welcome to Contractor Management, where you can manage the contractor qualification process for your company.

Create templates to manage different types of qualification requests

Maintain the qualification templates for your contractor pre-qualification processes in ‘Contractor Qualification Templates’.

Click ‘create new item’ to create a qualification template for a specific area of work. Open the qualification, where you can describe the work and later link the relevant documentation templates for each.

Define which documents are required per template

‘Requested Documents (Templates)’ allows you to describe and manage all documentation templates needed from your contractors.

Click ‘create new item’ to describe the type of document needed. When finished filling out the fields, click ‘create & open’. This navigates to the overview page where you can describe the requested document.

These templates can then be linked to one or more contractor templates, so that contractors know which documents to provide for this request.

Create a New Qualification Request

Once a qualification is described, you can begin sending requests to contractors in ‘Contractor Requests’.

Click ‘create new item’ to describe a qualification request. Then click ‘create & open’ to navigate to the qualification and specify the work and documents needed. Click ‘send request’ to submit the request to a contractor, who will then begin working on your request.

Follow-up on Existing Requests

Pending qualification requests can be monitored in ‘Pending Qualification Requests’, where you can accept or reject a contractor and follow up on the progress.

Click on an item to review a contractor’s input and documents. Here you can accept a contractor or ask for additional information.

Request Additional Info

At any time, you can also request extra information or documents from a contractor. Click on a qualification request to request more documents.

For requests you already approved, first click ‘Re-activate request’ before adding a document. After updating the request, the contractor will be notified and begin working on it.
